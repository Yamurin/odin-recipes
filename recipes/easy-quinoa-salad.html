Easy Quinoa Salad

Description

This quinoa salad is easy to make for a great summer meal. Light and citrusy, it's a whole new way to enjoy quinoa. Lime juice and cilantro give a refreshing kick, while quinoa and black beans make it hearty and filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Bring water and quinoa to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until quinoa is tender and water has been absorbed, 10 to 15 minutes. Set aside to cool.
